<h3 align="justify">
    I am currently pursuing B.Tech in AI & Intelligent Systems at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, India. My passion lies at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and Blockchain technology, where I build real-world applications that solve meaningful problems.<br /><br />
    My journey in AI spans across Large Language Models (LLMs), Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipelines, Computer Vision, and Natural Language Processing. I have built projects like an AI-powered Pitch Deck Generator using multi-agent architectures, a privacy-first Local AI Agent that runs entirely on-device, and PestVid — an AI-powered pest detection system using deep learning and computer vision.<br /><br />
    On the Blockchain side, I have developed smart contract-based peer-to-peer transaction systems on Ethereum, crypto time-lock encryption mechanisms, and Secure-DroneNet — a blockchain-secured drone communication network that combines IoT sensor data with on-chain verification for tamper-proof operations.<br /><br />
    I am also deeply interested in IoT and embedded systems. I built a Voice-Controlled Home Automation System using Arduino and designed RadioRover for radio communication applications. My work with Raspberry Pi, Arduino, and MQTT protocols has given me hands-on experience bridging the physical and digital worlds.<br /><br />
    In the fintech space, I developed a Payout Engine using Django, Celery, PostgreSQL and React — handling merchant payouts with robust concurrency control, idempotency, and state machine enforcement.<br /><br />
    Beyond coding, I am an enthusiast of competitive problem-solving and continuously exploring emerging technologies. I believe that AI combined with Web3 will shape the future of trustless, decentralized intelligence.<br /><br />
    In a nutshell, my journey has been a tapestry of academic rigor, hands-on engineering, and a relentless curiosity to build products that matter.
</h3>
